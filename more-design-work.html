UI Projects

Skate Pro

This app is for all the people who are interested in skating and helps them to be familiar with its different fields, learn them, track their skating activity and buy skate shoes or accessories.

Lamiz Coffee

This app aims to provide better services for customers of Lamiz coffee branches to encourage them to come in hours of solitude or make some items from the Lamiz menu in their homes.

StyLAW

StyLAW is a fashion adviser website that helps people choose and buy suitable clothes based on their appearance and where they want to go. The main goal is to make people stylish, and it allows users, like professional stylists, to choose the best item for all occasions.

Industrial Design Projects

DORSA Bed

This was a project for Dorsa Home Company and I searched about the materials they use and their product production process. Their target group are almost wealthy people. They wanted me to design a bed which reminds us of the Post-Modern style. Modelled in 3D MAX & Rendered in KEYSHOT.

Persian Restaurant Table

The aims of project were Improving the ergonomics in Persian Traditional Restaurant, decreasing the equipment costs, and paying attention to the demands of different customers when eating. First, we designed the shape of table and then we removed one of the sides of the octagon for easier use of it. Modelled in 3D MAX & Rendered in KEYSHOT.

Circo Hairdryer

The aim was to improve the ergonomics of the hair dryer. Inspired by a finger brush. The target community of this project are Kids and old people who have problems with hair dryer weight and also those who have short hair. Modelled in 3D MAX & Rendered in KEYSHOT.

Darkhoongah Photo Collection

Darkhoongah is one of the oldest neighbourhoods of Tehran. I collected them in the winter & spring of 2018.
