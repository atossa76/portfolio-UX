About me

Hi, I am Zahra Tahanan

UX Researcher & designer, based in London

My UX journey started with curiosity, self-learning, and personal projects in design. Over time, I found myself drawn to research, diving deeper into how people interact with technology. I studied industrial design in Tehran, where I learnt problem-solving and user-centred thinking. That passion eventually led me to Goldsmiths, where I recently graduated with an MSc in UX Engineering; blending research, design, and tech to create better experiences for people.

Two random things that I'm crazy about are cats and skates. I do Roller-skating through life’s challenges with a mantra: ‘This too shall pass,’ because I’ve overcome much harder turns.
